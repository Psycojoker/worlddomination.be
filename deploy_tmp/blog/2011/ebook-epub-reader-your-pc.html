An epurate ebook (epub) reader for your pc

From time to time I like to be able to read e-books (epub/mobi) on my pc. I wasn't satisfied by fbreader. The interface and customisations possibility are really cools, but the rendering kinda sucks (and doesn't works well for <span> tags that contain source code, this make unreadable most of my home made programming e-books).

After some research I've found lucidor. Doesn't look fantastic from a first view but the rendering is good.
