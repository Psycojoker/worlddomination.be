A small post to said that Romain and I just released a new version of FeedsTail that is compatible with python 2.5.

Note that the formating format is the one of python 2.5. So you'll have to do something like this:

feedstail -u http://reddit.com/.rss -f "%(title)s - %(link)s"

Except additional releases in the next days.
